Setup · AI Provider · Default

Apple Intelligence

On supported devices with Apple Intelligence enabled, Daily Kairos uses it automatically as the default AI provider — no API key or account required. Everything runs on-device, privately.

📋 Requirements

Apple Intelligence requires all of the following:

✓

Supported device — iPhone 15 Pro, iPhone 15 Pro Max, or any iPhone 16 model; iPad with an M1 chip or later.

✓

iOS / iPadOS 18.1 or later — Go to Settings → General → Software Update to check.

✓

Device language set to English (US) — Apple Intelligence is currently available in English only.

✓

Apple Intelligence enabled in Settings (see steps below).

⚙️ Enabling Apple Intelligence

If Apple Intelligence is not yet enabled on your device, go to Settings → Apple Intelligence & Siri and turn it on. If prompted, follow the on-screen steps to download the required model — this takes a few minutes over Wi-Fi.

How to enable Apple Intelligence — Apple Support ↗

📱 How it works in Daily Kairos

Once Apple Intelligence is enabled, open Daily Kairos. The app detects it automatically and sets it as the active AI provider — no API key, no account, no extra configuration needed.

Look up any Bible passage and reflections are generated entirely on-device. Responses feel snappy because nothing is sent to an external server.

To switch to a different provider at any time, tap the Settings icon (⚙️) → AI Provider and choose another option.

Private by design
When using Apple Intelligence, your Bible passages and generated reflections never leave your device. No data is sent to Apple or any third-party server. This is the most private way to use Daily Kairos.
⚠️ Device not supported?
If your device does not meet the requirements above, Apple Intelligence will not appear as an option. Choose one of the cloud AI providers instead — OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini, or a Custom provider.
