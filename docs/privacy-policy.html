Privacy Policy

Daily Kairos — Last updated May 1, 2025

Daily Kairos is a personal Bible study app. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The developer has no server and receives no data from your device.

Data Stored on Your Device

The following is stored locally on your device only:

  • Your reading history and streak data
  • Your AI provider preferences
  • Your ESV API key and any third-party AI provider API keys (stored in the iOS Keychain)

This data does not leave your device except in the two cases described below.

Data Sent to External Services

ESV Bible Text

Bible verse text is fetched from the ESV API (Crossway). Your ESV API key and the Bible references you look up are sent to Crossway's servers to retrieve passage text. See Crossway's privacy policy.

Third-Party AI Providers

If you choose to enable AI study features, the Bible references and passage text you are studying are sent directly from your device to the AI provider you select:

  • Anthropic Claude — anthropic.com/privacy
  • OpenAI — openai.com/privacy
  • Google Gemini — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Custom providers — subject to the privacy policy of the provider you configure

If you use On-Device AI (Apple Intelligence), all processing happens entirely on your device and nothing is sent to any external server.

AI provider API keys are stored only in the iOS Keychain and are never sent to anyone other than the provider you configured.

Share Feature

If you use the in-app share feature, the passage text and any content you choose to share is passed to iOS's standard share sheet. Where that content goes depends on the app or service you share it to (Mail, Messages, social media, etc.).

No Tracking or Analytics

The app contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising, no crash reporting services, and no third-party tracking of any kind.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.
